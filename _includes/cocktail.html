Cran-baby Sparkler

To celebrate, we'll be enjoying this refreshing signature cocktail at the event.
Gather these ingredients in advance so we can all be sipping on the same thing together!

    INGREDIENTS
  • Ice
  • 6 ounces of cranberry juice
  • Juice of one lime
  • 2 ounces sparkling water
  • Orange wedge for garnish
    DIRECTIONS
  1. Fill a highball glass with crushed ice.
  2. Add the lime juice and cranberry juice.
  3. Top with sparkling water and add an orange wedge for garnish.
  4. Optional: Spike with gin, vodka, or your favorite type of alcohol.
